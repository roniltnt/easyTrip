Paris, the City of Light! Here are three popular tourist attractions in Paris, each with a short description:

  • The Eiffel Tower: An iconic symbol of Paris. Visit for amazing views and dinner at the Le 58 Tour Eiffel.
  • The Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of masterpieces. The glass pyramid is a must-see.
  • Disneyland Paris: Perfect for families and kids. Enjoy thrilling rides, shows, and Disney magic.
